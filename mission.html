Primary Mission Overview

Our primary mission follows the standard CanSat requirements. We will be collecting and transmitting atmospheric data including temperature, pressure, and altitude during descent from 670m.

Secondary Mission Overview

Our secondary mission is to create an emergency contact radio system using LoRa communication to relay messages, location, and emergency info through the CanSat and forwarded back to home base. This mission introduces the idea of launching many communication “checkpoints” to provide data to those on dangerous adventures in case of an emergency. Our team plans to test the limits of LoRa and take advantage of the low power long range communication through an aerial device.
